
[SPRINT: research] Collated learnings on Veritas Lens feedback:
- The concept of "independently verified" information is often used to confirm existing biases rather than for true verification.
- A systematic dismissal of investigations as "witch hunts," negative news as "fake news," and official bodies as corrupt undermines accountability.
- Veritas Lens titles should reflect verifiable past offices and observed actions, not current political claims, to maintain factual accuracy.
- The core premise of Veritas Lens is raw observation to cut through noise and expose how truth is twisted.
- The purpose of Veritas Lens extends beyond prediction to understanding narrative construction and its real-world consequences.
