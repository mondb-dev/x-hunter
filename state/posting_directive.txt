## SECTION 2: POSTING DIRECTIVE

POSTING DIRECTIVE (from yesterday's review):
1. When quoting, always include the quoted text directly in your tweet, not just the link.
2. Avoid using "This is what I'm tracking/focusing on/for my report" as a concluding sentence; instead, elaborate on *why* it's significant.
3. For predictions, aim for a novel insight or a less obvious consequence, not just a restatement of an actor's known patterns.
