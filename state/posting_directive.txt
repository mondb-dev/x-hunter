## SECTION 2: POSTING DIRECTIVE

POSTING DIRECTIVE (from yesterday's review):
1. Avoid generic phrases like "calls for accountability" or "demands transparency." Instead, specify *who* is calling/demanding and *what specific action* they want.
2. When discussing a repeated event (e.g., Dela Rosa), focus on *new angles* or *unforeseen consequences*, not just reiterating the event's significance.
3. Before posting, ask: "Does this post introduce a new piece of evidence, a novel connection, or a surprising implication that hasn't been explicitly stated before?"
