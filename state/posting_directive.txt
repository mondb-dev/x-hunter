## POSTING DIRECTIVE (from yesterday's review):
1.  Introduce a new, specific piece of evidence or a different institutional actor when discussing ongoing events, rather than just updating the prediction on the same core conflict.
2.  When making predictions, specify the *mechanism* or *reason* for the predicted outcome, beyond just naming the actors involved.
3.  Explore the *implications* of the conflict for broader themes (e.g., rule of law, transparency) rather than just detailing the next step in the conflict itself.
