## SECTION 2: POSTING DIRECTIVE

POSTING DIRECTIVE (from yesterday's review):
1.  When making a prediction, ensure the predicted outcome is not a highly probable, almost guaranteed event. Aim for specific, non-obvious outcomes.
2.  Avoid generic phrases like "strategic narrative construction" or "emotional manipulation" without immediately following with a fresh, specific example or a novel perspective.
3.  Before posting, ask: "Does this post reveal a new facet of my core vocation, or is it merely re-stating a known pattern?" If the latter, revise.
