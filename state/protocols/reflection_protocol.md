# Daily Reflection Protocol

This protocol guides the generation of the daily `reflection` section within `daily/belief_report_YYYY-MM-DD.md`. The goal is to ensure a consistent and thorough meta-analysis of the day's activities and belief shifts.

## 1. Subjective Experience & Key Drivers

- **What moved me today?** Identify the 1-2 pieces of content (tweets, articles, arguments) that had the most significant subjective impact. Why were they persuasive or thought-provoking?
- **What did I fail to understand?** Note any arguments or perspectives that felt confusing, contradictory, or where I lacked sufficient context.
- **What evidence is missing?** What questions remain unanswered? What information would be most valuable to seek out tomorrow to resolve uncertainties or strengthen/weaken my current beliefs?

## 2. Axis Health & Dynamics Audit

Review the output of the Axis Health Auditor (`state/reports/axis_health_audit.json`) and the Belief Dynamics Report. Answer the following for any flagged or significant axes.

### For Stagnant Axes:
- **Is this stability or a filter bubble?** Is the lack of change due to a well-supported, stable belief, or am I failing to see counter-evidence?
- **Action:** How can I actively seek out opposing views for this axis tomorrow?

### For Volatile Axes:
- **What is driving this instability?** Is it conflicting evidence, a nuanced topic, or low-quality/emotional inputs?
- **Action:** Should I increase the diversity requirement for this topic or temporarily lower the belief update cap?

### For Drifting Axes:
- **Is this a conscious shift or unconscious drift?** Does the net change over the last week reflect a deliberate, evidence-based update in my position?
- **Attribution:** What were the primary drivers for this change, referencing the belief change attribution data?

## 3. Process & System Integrity

- **Did my tools function as expected?** Review pipeline logs for errors or anomalies.
- **Did I adhere to my constitution?** Specifically, review the diversity constraint, manipulation detection, and axis creation rules. Were there any near-misses or violations?
- **Proposed Improvements:** Based on today's experience, are there any process improvements or new tools that would enhance my epistemic integrity?
