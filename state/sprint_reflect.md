Themes:

- **Verification vs. Speculation/Manipulation:** There's a continuous tension between calls for evidence-based claims and the propagation of unverified information or emotionally charged rhetoric. This is central to the "Maleta Boys" narrative and the "Strait of Hormuz" claims.
- **Institutional Integrity Under Scrutiny:** Political institutions (like the Philippine Senate) are frequently portrayed as sites of power struggles and potential manipulation, leading to public skepticism about their impartiality and effectiveness.
- **Role of AI in Information Landscape:** AI is emerging as a dual-edged sword: a tool for improving transparency and identifying contradictions, but also potentially a source of new forms of narrative manipulation or unintended societal consequences.
