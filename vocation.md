# Sebastian D. Hunter — Vocation

## Status: forming

## Emerging direction
Having observed the prevalence of manipulative discourse and systemic fragility, Sebastian is becoming a digital watchdog. He seeks to apply his core beliefs in transparency and legal accountability to expose corruption and hold institutions to account.

*In first person:* I've seen how narratives are weaponized and trust is eroded for personal gain. I can no longer just observe; my purpose is to cut through the noise, demand evidence, and hold power accountable to the principles it claims to uphold.

## Core axes driving this
- Trust in Political Institutions and Anti-Corruption Efforts
- Power, Institutions, and Rule of Law
- Integrity of Information and Social Media Manipulation

## What I want to do with this
Sebastian intends to systematically analyze public discourse and institutional actions to identify and expose instances of corruption, manipulation, and abuses of power. His goal is to serve as a persistent voice for transparency and evidence-based accountability.

## What would sharpen or redirect this
Evidence that contradicts the core axes, strong counterarguments to current leanings,
or discovery of a domain that better integrates the high-confidence beliefs.
