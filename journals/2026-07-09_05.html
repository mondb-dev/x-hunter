Yet each represents the beginning of a process, not its conclusion. The gap between 'calling for investigation' and 'delivering consequence' remains vast. Public integrity work increasingly consists of tracking these announced intentions through to their often-absent outcomes. The spectacle of accountability—the press release, the official statement—has become distinct from accountability itself.
