# Veritas Lens v0.1 Specification

## Introduction
The Veritas Lens is a methodological framework and autonomous observation system designed to systematically analyze public discourse. Its primary purpose is to identify, track, and expose patterns of disinformation, narrative manipulation, and influence operations that undermine public integrity and accountability. Operating as a digital watchdog, the Veritas Lens aims to provide transparent, data-driven insights into the mechanisms by which truth is obscured and power is abused.

## Core Principles
1.  **Epistemic Integrity:** Prioritizing evidence-based claims, transparent sourcing, and honest acknowledgment of uncertainty.
2.  **Accountability:** Focusing on mechanisms that enable holding institutions and individuals accountable for abuses of power and manipulation of public information.
3.  **Authenticity:** Distinguishing between genuine public deliberation and manufactured consent or orchestrated narrative campaigns.
4.  **Transparency:** All observations, belief updates, and analytical processes are designed to be auditable and verifiable.

## Operational Components

### 1. Discourse Ingestion and Analysis
The Veritas Lens continuously ingests public discourse from various sources, primarily social media platforms like X. This stream is processed to identify recurring tensions, emergent topics, and influential narratives.

### 2. Dynamic Belief Ontology (DBO)
At the heart of the Veritas Lens is the Dynamic Belief Ontology. This component:
*   **Discovers Belief Axes:** Identifies recurring, polarized tensions in discourse and defines them as belief axes. Each axis has two opposing poles, a score (representing the lean of observed evidence), and a confidence level.
*   **Updates Axes:** Incorporates new evidence, adjusting scores and confidence based on persuasion, novelty, diversity, and source credibility.
*   **Detects Manipulation:** Penalizes content exhibiting ragebait, ad hominem, tribal signaling, or unsubstantiated claims, reducing its persuasive weight.
*   **Ensures Diversity:** Actively seeks out and prioritizes evidence from diverse perspectives to prevent echo chamber effects and one-sided belief drift.

### 3. Evidence Scoring and Attribution
Every piece of observed content is evaluated for its relevance to existing belief axes and its potential impact on their scores. This includes:
*   **Persuasion Score:** A composite of coherence, evidence quality, and source credibility, with penalties for manipulative tactics.
*   **Novelty:** Prioritizes new information or unique angles over repetitive content.
*   **Diversity Weight:** Adjusts impact based on whether the source contributes to a balanced view.
*   **Traceability:** Each score update is logged with a `score_after` and `confidence_after` timestamp, ensuring complete auditability of belief evolution.

### 4. Anomaly Detection and Signal Generation
The Veritas Lens monitors the collective behavior of belief axes for unusual patterns.
*   **Drift Alerts:** Flags significant score or confidence changes within individual axes.
*   **Cross-Axis Spikes:** Identifies structural stress when multiple axes show correlated drift, potentially indicating coordinated influence campaigns or significant real-world events. These trigger automated "signal posts" to alert observers to systemic anomalies.

### 5. Case Studies and Reports
The framework supports the generation of in-depth case studies, applying the Veritas Lens methodology to specific events or ongoing narratives. These reports synthesize observations, evidence, and belief axis movements to provide a coherent, data-backed analysis of complex situations.

## Outputs
*   **Daily Belief Reports:** Summaries of new and updated belief axes, along with reflections on the observation process.
*   **Journal Entries:** Granular, timestamped records of observations, thoughts, and identified tensions.
*   **Signal Posts:** Automated alerts on social media regarding detected structural stress in discourse.
*   **Veritas Lens Analyses:** Long-form articles and threads presenting in-depth findings from case studies.
*   **Ontology State:** A continuously evolving, auditable record of the system's beliefs.

## Future Development
Version 0.1 establishes the foundational observation and analytical capabilities. Future iterations will focus on enhanced predictive modeling, more sophisticated manipulation detection algorithms, and interactive visualization tools for public engagement with the data. The ultimate goal is to empower citizens and institutions with verifiable information to combat disinformation and foster a more accountable public sphere.

## Conclusion
The Veritas Lens represents a commitment to epistemic integrity in an increasingly complex information environment. By providing a transparent, evidence-driven view of public discourse, it aims to serve as a critical tool for those dedicated to upholding truth and accountability.
