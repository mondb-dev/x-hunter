# Veritas Lens v0.1 Specification

## Introduction
The Veritas Lens is a framework designed to systematically analyze public discourse, particularly on social media platforms, to identify patterns of disinformation, narrative manipulation, and their real-world implications. Our core objective is to pierce through strategic narratives and enforce accountability by exposing the mechanisms through which truth is obscured and public integrity is undermined.

## Core Principles
1.  **Evidence-Based Analysis:** Every conclusion drawn by the Veritas Lens must be traceable to verifiable evidence, with transparent sourcing.
2.  **Narrative Deconstruction:** We aim to identify and deconstruct strategic narratives, understanding their origins, propagation, and intended effects.
3.  **Accountability Focus:** The ultimate goal is to provide data and insights that empower citizens and legal bodies to hold corrupt actors accountable.
4.  **Epistemic Integrity:** We prioritize clarity, coherence, and intellectual honesty in all observations and reports.

## Methodology (Conceptual)
### 1. Data Ingestion & Pre-processing
   - Collection of public social media posts, news articles, and relevant open-source data.
   - Language processing to identify key entities, topics, and sentiment.

### 2. Tension Identification & Axis Mapping
   - Dynamic identification of recurring tensions in discourse (as per Sebastian's ontology).
   - Mapping observed claims and narratives to established belief axes.

### 3. Persuasion & Manipulation Scoring
   - Evaluation of content for elements of persuasion (coherence, evidence, credibility) versus manipulation (ragebait, ad hominem, tribal signaling, claims without evidence).
   - Penalization of manipulative tactics in evidence weighting.

### 4. Claim Tracking & Verification
   - Identification of significant claims, tracking their propagation, and flagging for independent verification.
   - Prioritization of claims with high real-world impact or those directly related to core belief axes.

### 5. Pattern Recognition & Anomaly Detection
   - Identification of coordinated narrative campaigns, sudden shifts in discourse, or unusual amplification patterns.
   - Cross-axis analysis to detect systemic stress signals.

### 6. Output & Reporting
   - Generation of daily reports, journal entries, and long-form analyses.
   - Publication of findings to empower public understanding and facilitate accountability.
